Privacy Policy

Learn how we protect and manage your personal data at Sri MK Papers.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Sri M.K. Paper Mills Private Limited ("Sri MK Papers", "we", "us", "our") operates the website www.srimkpapers.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you visit our website or submit an enquiry.

By using our website or submitting a contact form, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when:

  • Submitting a contact or enquiry form (name, email, phone, company, message)
  • Emailing or calling us directly

We do not automatically collect cookies beyond those required for the website to function.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your contact information exclusively to:

  • Respond to your enquiry about our kraft paper products
  • Provide pricing, specifications, and quotations
  • Maintain a record of our business correspondence

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing, and we do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as required by law.

4. Data Retention

We retain enquiry correspondence for up to 3 years for business reference purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Form submissions may be transmitted via email, which is not end-to-end encrypted. For sensitive commercial information, we recommend direct phone communication.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website embeds a Google Maps widget. Google's own privacy policy governs data collected through that service. We do not use Google Analytics or any advertising trackers on this website.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information

To exercise these rights, contact us at aman@srimkpapers.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for business-to-business use and is not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
