Quality & Certifications

Our strict quality control parameters and international certifications ensure consistency, traceability, and sustainability in every reel.

QA Process

Our Quality Commitment

At Sri M.K. Paper Mills, quality is not an afterthought — it is embedded in every stage of our production process. From incoming raw material inspection to final reel dispatch, each batch undergoes systematic testing against our quality specifications.

Our ISO 9001:2015 certification validates our quality management system, ensuring consistent product quality, traceable processes, and continual improvement. Our FSC® Chain of Custody certification enables us to supply verified recycled paper for customers with sustainability procurement requirements.
